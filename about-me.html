ABOUT ME

I'm a 20 years old game design student in Rubika, Valenciennes.

 

I began playing videogames with my brother when I was young, and never stopped since.

 

At work: I do my best so everyone can get along even though they don't know eachothers and try to organize everyone's task so nobody has to work at the last minute and under pressure. I try to see everyone's strengths and create an environment where they can express themselves and do their best.

 

At home

 

I love traveling, I've had the chance to visit a lot of countries, amongst them my favorite ones are Jamaïca, Greece and Germany. People there have very different ways of thinking and living and it's always interesting to discover new ways to think.

 

 

I also love listening to music, mainly metal & rap, but I don't mind some reggae or French variety ! My favorite band is Rammstein, they are the one who made me pick German as second language. Their texts are beautiful, the rhythm is perfect and they often innovate with violins or such.

 

 

Books are something I used to read a lot, but since I've been in Rubika I kind of dropped them. Still reading during holidays, mostly philosophy.

 

 

Finally, when it comes to videogames I love games that propose something new or strong intentions

 

For example, my favorite game is Portal. When the game starts, you're curious about everything, Aperture's lab doesn't look like anything you've seen in games. GLaDOS is my favorite antagonist of all times, both your friend and the most sarcastic enemy you can find. Every dialogue is perfect, you can feel the devs playing with you all along. The portals are an awesome mechanic, and the game lasts long enough for you to understand everything you can do, and yet doesn't last too long so you don't get bored.
