ABOUT ME

In this game your goal is simple: grab as much marbles as possible !

 

When the game starts, someone has to take the Dumper™ and release all the marbles it contains on the board. Each player then grabs his or her Controller™ and starts running around the table to collect the marbles. The Board™ where the marbles bounce can tilt, making it possible to change the course of the marbles !

 

Created by: Nicolas Bekhazi, Valentin Bourgogne, Lila Grimaldi, Martin Jacob, Alexis Jouanneau.

Special thanks to : Eddy Sarot & Tatiana Vilela dos Santos

 

Stuff used:

- lots of wood and screws

- a bed

- a jean

- marbles

 

 

The goal was to make a game where player can interact with eachother physically, to create something that could have its place in a pub just like a pinball or a table soccer.

 

My tasks were to, well, craft the piece and the creation of the blueprints we would use to do it. I also made the "commercial" video, with a music made by Romain Tondre.

I did a Seminar for Renault where we had to design the future's autonomous car. I can't tell much about it for now but it was a great experience since I had to work in a field I'm not used to.

 

Our goal was to design a car that would be interesting since it doesn't need any driver, and we tried to avoid the classical "put games on the windows" answer. Since we mostly came from different fields, it was really interesting to brainstorm and see everyone bring opinions and knowledge in order to have a vehicle that pleased us all.

Inquisition is a roleplay game in which you play someone that has been imprisoned by the Spanish Inquisition. You have to escape and try to abolish the Inquisition by rallying the crowds to your cause and killing or dismissing Torquemada, the Inquisition's leader.

 

Our goal was to design a roleplay game where you weren't the hack'n'slash hero that kills everyone that stands on his way but someone part of a society where there is fear and totalitarism.
